Meet Dr Mandi

Cosmetic Physician

Dr Mandi holds an MBBS degree and is a Fellow of the Royal Australian College of General Practitioners (FRACGP). With a background in anatomy tutoring at Bond University and advanced training across Gold Coast, Sydney, and Melbourne, she brings both depth and precision to cosmetic medicine.

Her approach is holistic - looking at your whole face, your lifestyle, and what matters to you before recommending a single treatment. No cookie-cutter plans here. Every consultation is a conversation about what would genuinely help you feel more like yourself.

Based in Caroline Springs, Dr Mandi's cosmetic skin clinic is a calm, welcoming space where you can explore your options without any pressure. Whether it's anti-wrinkle treatments, skin rejuvenation, or simply wanting to feel refreshed - you're in experienced, caring hands.
