Questions You Might Be Thinking

Most people describe it as very manageable - similar to a light scratching sensation. We use a topical numbing cream before your treatment to keep you as comfortable as possible. Many of our patients say it was much easier than they expected.
Most people see beautiful results in 2 - 3 sessions, spaced about 4 - 6 weeks apart. During your consultation, Dr Mandi will recommend a plan tailored to your skin and your goals. Some people notice a glow after just one session.
PRF is the next generation of platelet therapy. Unlike PRP, PRF doesn't use anticoagulants - which means it releases growth factors slowly over a longer period, giving your skin more time to heal and regenerate. Think of it as a slower, deeper repair.
You can expect results to build over 3 - 6 months as your skin continues to regenerate. Many patients enjoy the benefits for up to a year. A maintenance session every 6 - 12 months helps keep your glow going.
You might have some mild redness for a day or two - similar to a light sunburn. Most people go back to their normal routine the next day. We'll give you simple aftercare instructions to make sure your skin heals beautifully.
